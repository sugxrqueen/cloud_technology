Meet the protagonists!

This is Shuichi Saihara (sigh-ha-ra) the Ultimate Detective, and Kaede Akamatsu the Ultimate Pianist. They are both the protagonists of the third game as one is changed for the other. This is another reason why this game is more special than the others as this has two protagonists and the others have one. This game consists of wrapping up the two prior games while revealing a hidden truth of the Danganronpa Franchise.
