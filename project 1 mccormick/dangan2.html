Meet the protagonist!

This is Hajime Hinata. His talent is unknown and it's a plot twist toward the middle of the game. He is quite snappy and quick-witted. This is why he's my personal favorite protagonist. Him and the rest of his classmates go through the killing game, students dropping one by one. Meanwhile they unconver forgotten secrets of the first game.
