Danganronpa!

Danganronpa (daang-gone-ronpa) is a three main part game that consists of 16 highschool students, each with exceptional 'Ultimate' talents. In Danganronpa, the highschool students are forced to kill each other to depict which is the most superior talent amongst them. This theme repeats for all three games.

The Three Main Games!

Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc

This is the first game in the Danganronpa franchise. The protagonist in this game is Makoto Naegi, the Ultimate Lucky Student. The students adapt to the school environment while adjusting to this killing game they're trapped in.

Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair

This is the second game in the Danganronpa franchise. The protagonist in this game is Hajime Hinata, his Ultimate Talent is unknown The students are put onto this island to murder each other while uncovering the secrets of the prior game.

Learn More!

Danganronpa V3: Killing Harmony

This is the third and final game in the Danganronpa franchise. The protagonists in this game is Kaede Akamastu, the Ulitmate Pianist and Shuichi Saihara, the Ultimate Detective. This game is my personal favorite out of the three because of it's quirkiness. This game adds closure to the lore of the last two while adding a major plot twist at the end.
