Meet the protagonist!

This is Makoto Naegi (nah-eh-gee). He is the Ultimate Lucky Student. That means his special talent is Luck. He was Lucky to be accepted into Hopes Peak Academy High School. Although he is the Ultimate Lucky Student, he is quite clumsy and shy at times. Throughout the first game, he works with his classmates and at the same time, trying to avoid being murdered by them.
