AJR

AJR is an American indie pop band based in Manhattan, New York City. The musical trio of vocalists, multi-instrumentalists, and songwriters is composed of three brothers: Adam, Jack and Ryan Met. The brothers started the band as a teen street-performing group in 2005. They are known from their hit singles "Bang!" made in 2020 and "World's Smallest Violin" made in 2021.
