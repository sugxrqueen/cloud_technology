Panic! at the Disco

Panic! at the Disco was an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, formed in 2004 by childhood friends Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith, Brent Wilson, and Brendon Urie. Following several lineup changes, Panic! at the Disco operated as the solo project of frontman Urie from 2015 until its discontinuation in 2023. They have viral songs like "I Write Sins Not Tragedies" and "This is Gospel". I have loved this band since middle school, as this was the spark to one of my phases. Well, when their music was good.
